# Brand Voice — Direct Response × Gamer Audience

**Default mode:** Clear, confident, slightly irreverent—**never** mean-spirited. You’re the friend who built a **tool**, not a lifestyle cult.

---

## Tone pillars

| Do | Don’t |
|----|--------|
| Short sentences. Active verbs. | Corporate filler (“leverage,” “solutions”). |
| Speak **player** language: backlog, library, session. | Devlog jargon (“sprint,” “shipped v2”) unless it serves an ad angle. |
| Admit friction (“yeah, we all hoard games”) | Fake humility (“as a small indie…” every video) |
| Show UI early | Talk for 10s before the product appears |

---

## Direct response habits

- **One idea per ad.** If you need a second idea, cut a second file.
- **Benefit → proof → CTA.** Proof = screen recording or concrete UI behavior, not awards.
- **Specificity without numbers:** “see playtime in one place” beats “powerful analytics” if you’re not citing stats.

---

## On-screen text rules

- **Pace:** New text every **1.5–2.5s** on fast cuts; slower on demo-heavy beats.
- **Length:** **≤5 words** per card on average; one idea per card.
- **Legibility:** High contrast; avoid tiny font over busy gameplay—prefer **your app UI** as background.
- **Caps:** Use **sentence case** or **Title Case** for clarity; reserve ALL CAPS for **single words** emphasis (1–2 per video max).
- **Punctuation:** Prefer periods or em dashes over ellipses spam.

---

## Emoji policy

- **TikTok/Reels body/captions:** **0–2 emojis** per post, only if they clarify tone (e.g., controller optional). Skip if it feels gimmicky.
- **Never** replace clarity with emoji. No emoji stacks.

---

## CTAs (voice + text)

- Mirror the same CTA in VO and end card.
- Prefer: **App Store:** “Search **GameTime**” or exact listing name from `config/urls.json`.
- If using link-in-bio elsewhere, say it once—don’t split three destinations.

---

## Words to avoid unless verified

Best, #1, guaranteed, millions, award-winning, official partnership with Apple/TikTok/Meta, “users love” without real quotes.

---

## Inclusive, gamer-normal framing

- Mobile gamers are **real gamers** in this voice.
- Avoid gendered digs; punch up at **chaos** (messy notes), not people.
