# Compliance — Ads, App Store, Copyright, Privacy

This is **guidance**, not legal advice. When in doubt, **omit** or **verify** before publishing.

---

## App Store & marketing honesty

- Marketing copy and creatives must **match what the app actually does** in regions where it’s available.
- Do **not** promise features still in development unless clearly framed as coming soon **and** allowed by platform rules.
- Do **not** imply Apple, TikTok, Instagram, or IGDB **endorsement**—no logos as “featured by” unless you have written permission.
- **No fabricated metrics:** downloads, rankings, revenue, session counts, star averages—use live, checkable facts only.

---

## Copyright — games, art, footage, audio

- **Prefer:** Screen recordings of **your app’s UI** and **your** demo library assets.
- **Game publishers’ trailers, key art, and OST** are usually **protected**. Using them in ads can trigger takedowns or strikes—even if “everyone does it.”
- **Safer patterns:**
  - Show **your** interface listing a title **without** making publisher art the hero frame.
  - Use **original** B-roll (hands on phone, desk, mic) and **licensed** or **original** music.
- **IGDB/metadata:** Use in line with IGDB/consumer API terms and your app’s implementation—don’t imply you “own” game artwork displayed through metadata if rights are limited. GameTime shows **IGDB attribution** in-app—on-camera mentions or B-roll of that UI can support **trust**; still no false “official partnership” with IGDB/Twitch/etc. unless contracted.

If you must reference a game **by name**, factual naming is generally fine; **visual** use of their assets is the higher-risk area.

---

## Privacy — what not to show

- **No real user libraries, usernames, emails, or precise locations** in screen capture.
- Use a **demo account** or sanitized data; label on screen if helpful: “Demo library.”
- Avoid showing **notifications** from other apps, **message previews**, or **personal photos**.

---

## Contests & UGC (if you add later)

- Disclose material connection; follow platform rules for promotions.
- Get written permission for reposting user content.

---
